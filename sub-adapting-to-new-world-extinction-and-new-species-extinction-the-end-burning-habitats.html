Lizards love sun basking! On a sunny day in most parts of the world, you'll see them out enjoying the sunshine. But even they can get too much of a good thing. When it gets too hot they tend to retreat back into the shade.

Over the last few decades, Sceloporus lizards in Mexico have had a problem: it has been too hot too often and they have been spending more time hidden under their rocks. Not able to venture out for food or to find their friends, many of these populations have perished. While they have been trying to adapt and get used to their warmer world, the pace of change has been too fast for them. Many other lizard species have been struggling with this same burning issue.
