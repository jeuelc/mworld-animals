Sharks, Rays and Us

Humans have travelled across oceans and relied on them for food for thousands of years. Sharks and rays are an integral part of the ocean, keeping food webs in check and in turn helping many civilisations to prosper.

Many island cultures that rely almost entirely on the ocean for food have a great respect for the shark and recognise their importance. Sharks are worshipped as gods and form a big part of their culture and way of life.

In other cultures, sharks are popularised as cold killing machines. Examples of this can be seen in Hollywood films like 'Jaws' and in news reports of shark attacks. In the modern era, our demand on the ocean for food has increased enormously and as a consequence, fishing has caused population crashes in about 90% of shark and rays globally!
