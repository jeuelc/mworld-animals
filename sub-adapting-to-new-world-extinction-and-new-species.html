Extinctions and New Species

Animals can never relax when it comes to adaptation. Your environment is constantly changing, and you will need to do likewise - much like running on a treadmill! Whether your food sources are dwindling or parasites and predators are moving into your territory, if you stop adapting you lag behind. Those species not up to the task fall back and may eventually become extinct. In contrast, species that are good at change may even split into multiple species. We call this 'speciation'. This happens when individuals change so much that they can no longer reproduce with animals who share a common ancestor.
